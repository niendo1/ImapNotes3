A free and fast notes editor for Android that allows for synchronization with IMAP servers.

<b>General Attributes</b>
<ul>
<li>compatible with various IMAP servers (Gmail, Yahoo, AOL, Posteo, and your server)</li>
<li>security options include plain text, SSL/TLS, or STARTTLS</li>
<li>save notes as standard HTML E-mails</li>
<li>manage multiple IMAP accounts and/or notepads</li>
<li>works offline (with background synchronization)</li>
</ul>

<b>Feature-Rich HTML Editor</b>
<ul>
<li>versatile text formatting</li>
<li>image integration</li>
<li>create checklists</li>
<li>full-text search</li>
<li>individual font and background colors</li>
</ul>

<b>Additional Capabilities</b>
<ul>
<li>no tracking or advertising</li>
<li>light and dark mode support</li>
<li>filter and organize your notes by #HashTags</li>
<li>note sharing with other apps (import/export)</li>
<li>backup notes as local zip archive</li>
<li>restore all or just a single note from a local archive</li>
</ul>
  
All of these features are contained in this fully open source app, which has no tracking or advertising, and requires only minimal user permissions.
